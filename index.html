My Fantasy Card Game

About My Cards

Welcome to my collection of fantasy cards! Each card represents powerful creatures, from a world of magic. Explore the rules below and dive into the details of each card.

Rules

1. Build a deck of 15 cards minimum.
2. Draw 7 cards at the start of the game.
3. Place 2 cards of your choosing down for battle.
4. Take turns attacking and replacing new cards as old ones are defeated
5. The goal is to defeat every card in your opponents deck.
6. Now prepare for battle and have fun!
